About Us

Business Overview

We are a video game distributor located in Irvine, CA. Our team is comprised of over 40 designers, artists, programmers, software engineers, accountants, businessmen, and HCI experts who are all dedicated to making our customers happy.

Types of Games

Our website is a web based store that sells digital copies of video games.

You can find games from many different genres, including action, puzzle, role-playing, platforming, strategy, and many more. We have a large variety of games that range from the biggest AAA to the humblest indie.
